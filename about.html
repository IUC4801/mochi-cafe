ABOUT US

Our obsession with great coffee comes from growing up in India, which has had a thriving espresso culture for two decades. We opened our first store in Termini, Rome as homage to the legacy and traditions of the country. And we’re based in Florence because it’s a city that rewards excellence, which is what we strive for in every coffee we serve. We wish we could be everyone’s neighbor.
